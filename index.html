Event Notice for 2020



Korea's largest music & camping festival 'Rainbow 2020' will be held on June 13 ~ 14 at 'Jara Island', a sacred camping place full of greenery!

The 2020 Rainbow Music & Camping Festival, celebrating its 10th anniversary, will be a camping healing festival where you can joy music in the beautiful Gapyeong nature and spend a day with your loved ones. Happy deviation of two days and one night! We look forward to your participation in Rainbow Music & Camping Festival.

The flea market spreads through the forest from day to night. Green recording, merchants more free than anyone, and audiences more free than anyone It is a pleasant place than anywhere else.

'Rainbow Music & Camping Festival 2019'
attracted 15,000 people



Korea's largest music & camping festival 'Rainbow Music & Camping Festival 2019' was held in great success, attracting a total of 15,000 spectators for 1 night and 2 days from June 1 to 2.

'Rainbow Music & Camping Festival 2019' held at Gapyeong Jara Island is a large-scale festival where music and camping coexist in the ninth year. By constructing the largest camping zone in Korea, the audience enjoyed a day's departure from the city center.
